Hero Section
Full-width first impression — must communicate instantly
Most critical
Full-screen Background Image
Rich photo of Ghanaian food: jollof rice spread, waakye, banku & tilapia, or beautifully plated event food. Dark overlay for text readability.
Headline
Bold statement. E.g. "Accra's Finest Catering for Every Occasion." Large, serif or bold display font.
Sub-headline
One line of supporting text. E.g. "Weddings · Corporate Events · Funerals · Naming Ceremonies." Highlight local event types.
Primary CTA Buttons
Two buttons: "View Packages" (primary) and "Get a Free Quote" (secondary). Both clearly visible on the hero image.
Quick Stats Bar
Below the headline — "500+ Events Served · 10 Years Experience · Fully Licensed." Builds instant trust.
Image Slideshow / Video
Auto-playing slider of event photos or a short background reel of food preparation and table setups.

Seasonal
Seasonal Promotion Text

Why Choose Us
Trust signals and key differentiators — who you are
Trust builder
Section Headline
E.g. "Why Accra Trusts Us With Their Most Important Days." Emotionally resonant heading.
Feature Icon Cards (4–6)
Each card has icon + title + short description. Topics: Fresh local ingredients · Experienced chefs · On-time delivery · Affordable pricing · FDA certified · Halal & non-Halal options.
Certifications & Badges
FDA Ghana badge, Health certificates, any culinary awards or association memberships. Trust is key in the Ghanaian market.

Short Brand Story Paragraph

4
Event Types We Cater
Show exactly which occasions you serve — recognition drives clicks
Key section
Weddings (Trad & White)
Both traditional Ghanaian weddings (knocking ceremony) and church/white weddings. Show setup photos for each.
Funerals & Outdoorings
Critical in Ghana — large funeral gatherings need mass catering. Be explicit. Same for outdooring (naming ceremonies).
Corporate Events & Launches
Office parties, product launches, NGO events, staff appreciation days, Accra's growing corporate scene.
Birthday & Private Parties
1st birthdays, 21st, 50th, and milestone celebrations. Show colourful table setups and dessert tables.
Church & Religious Events
Church anniversaries, harvest festivals, revival programs. Very common in Accra — address it directly.
School & Graduation Events
Graduation parties, PTA events, school prize-givings. A niche but loyal customer segment.
Card Layout Style
Each event type as a photo card with name overlay. Clicking each card scrolls or links to the matching package.
5
Packages & Pricing
The core booking section — must be very clear and easy to browse
Book here
Filter / Tab Bar
Tabs to filter by event type: "All · Wedding · Corporate · Funeral · Birthday." Reduces scroll fatigue.
Package Cards (3–4 tiers)
Basic / Standard / Premium / VIP. Each card shows: package name, guest count range, price per head or flat rate, list of what's included.
What's Included Checklist
Per package: Menu items (specify local dishes), Serving style (buffet/plated), Equipment (chafing dishes, chairs, etc.), Staff count, Setup/cleanup included.
Pricing Display
Show prices in GHS (Ghanaian Cedis). Option for "Starting from GHS 120 per head" if custom pricing varies. Transparency builds trust.
"Most Popular" Badge
Highlight the Standard or Premium package as most booked. Guides undecided customers to a choice.
"Book This Package" Button
Each card has a CTA that opens the booking form or WhatsApp pre-filled with the package name. No friction.
Custom / Bespoke Option
A final card: "Don't see what you need? Let's create a custom package." Links to contact form or WhatsApp.
Minimum Guest Notice
Clearly state minimum guest count (e.g. "We cater for a minimum of 50 guests"). Manages expectations upfront.

Menu Showcase
The food is the product — show it beautifully
Key draw
Category Tabs
Tabs: "Ghanaian Dishes · Continental · Grills & BBQ · Soups & Stews · Drinks & Juices · Desserts." Let users browse what they'll eat.
Food Photo Grid
High-quality photos of each dish with name and a short 1-line description. Jollof, waakye, kontomire, fried rice, banku. Photography quality matters hugely.
Dietary Labels
Small badges: Halal · Vegetarian · Contains Nuts · Spicy. Important for diverse Accra clientele including expats and Muslims.
Download Full Menu Button
PDF download of the complete menu with all dishes and descriptions. Also helps with sharing via WhatsApp.
Chef's Specialties Highlight
A "Chef Recommends" row or banner featuring 3 signature dishes with larger photos and a personal note from the chef.
Seasonal / Special Menu Note
E.g. "Special Christmas menu available November–January." Creates relevance and timely conversion.
7
Booking / Quote Request Form
The conversion engine — make it simple and frictionless
Converts here
Form Headline
E.g. "Get Your Free Quote in 24 Hours." Set expectations and reduce hesitation with a clear promise.
Full Name & Phone Number
Essential fields. Phone over email — Ghanaians respond faster to calls and WhatsApp than email.
Event Type Dropdown
Select from: Wedding · Funeral · Birthday · Corporate · Church Event · Other. Pre-selects context for the team.
Event Date & Location
Date picker + text field for venue/area in Accra. Helps the team plan logistics and check availability instantly.
Number of Guests Slider
Range slider from 50–2000 guests. Shows estimated price range dynamically as user adjusts — very engaging.
Package Selection
Radio buttons or dropdown for Basic / Standard / Premium / Custom. Optional — can be discussed after form submission.
Additional Notes Field
Free-text area: "Any dietary requirements, special menu requests, or event details." Captures custom needs.
Submit + WhatsApp Option
Two CTAs: "Submit Request" (form) and "Chat on WhatsApp Instead." Many Ghanaians prefer WhatsApp — never block this path.
Response Time Promise
Below the button: "We respond within 2 hours during business hours." Sets expectations and reduces abandonment.
8
Photo & Video Gallery
Show real events — social proof in visual form
Social proof
Masonry / Grid Layout
Pinterest-style photo grid with varying sizes — more visually interesting than uniform squares. Mix food close-ups with table setups and event shots.
Category Filters
Filter gallery by: "All · Weddings · Funerals · Corporate · Food Close-ups · Table Setup." Lets people see their event type specifically.
Lightbox on Click
Clicking any photo opens a full-screen lightbox with prev/next navigation. Standard but necessary UX.
Featured Video / Reel
A 1–2 min event recap video embedded above the photo grid. Video builds connection faster than photos alone.
Instagram Feed Integration
Live pull from Instagram showing most recent posts. Keeps the gallery always fresh without manual updates.
"View Full Gallery" Button
Show only 9–12 images on the homepage. Button leads to dedicated Gallery page with all event photos.

Testimonials & Reviews
Real customers, real words — highest trust signal in the Ghanaian market
Must have
Section Headline
E.g. "What Our Clients Say." Simple and direct works best. Avoid corporate language.
Review Cards (5–8)
Each card: Customer photo (or initials avatar) · Name · Event type · Star rating · Written review in their own words. Auto-scrolling carousel.
Local Community Names
Use real Ghanaian names like Ama Mensah, Kofi Asante. Ghanaian customers relate better to familiar names than Western ones.
Event Type Tag
Small badge on each review: "Wedding · Funeral · Birthday." Lets visitors find reviews for their specific event type.
Google Reviews Widget
Embed or link to Google Business rating. Shows overall star rating (e.g. 4.8★ from 120 reviews) for instant credibility.
Video Testimonials
1–2 short video clips (30–60 sec) of happy clients speaking. Ghanaian customers trust video more than written text.
10
How the Booking Process Works
Remove uncertainty — show customers the exact steps
Reduces friction
Step 1 — Choose Your Package
Browse packages and select the one matching your event type and guest count. Or request a custom quote.
Step 2 — Fill the Booking Form
Submit your event date, location, number of guests, and any special requirements. Takes less than 2 minutes.
Step 3 — Confirm with a Deposit
We send a quote. You pay a small deposit via Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo) to secure your date. Clearly state Momo is accepted.
Step 4 — We Handle Everything
Our team shows up at the venue, sets up, serves, and cleans up. You enjoy the event stress-free.
Visual Step Indicator
Numbered icons connected by a line or arrow. Each step has an icon, bold step number, title, and 2-line description.
Payment Methods Banner
Show accepted payments: MTN Mobile Money · Vodafone Cash · AirtelTigo · Cash · Bank Transfer. Very important for Ghanaian market.

13
Frequently Asked Questions
Answer objections before they become reasons not to book
Objection handler
Accordion Layout
Questions collapsed by default. Users click to expand answers. Keeps the page clean while making all info accessible.
Do you cater outside Accra?
Mention if you serve Kumasi, Tema, Takoradi, or other regions. Many clients outside Accra will look for this.
What is your minimum order?
State minimum guest count clearly (e.g., 50 guests). Saves time screening enquiries that don't match your capacity.
Do you provide equipment?
Tables, chairs, canopies, chafing dishes, serving spoons — specify what you provide vs what they must arrange separately.
Can I customize my menu?
Yes/No with explanation. Important for customers wanting specific dishes for cultural or religious reasons.
How far in advance should I book?
E.g. "We recommend booking at least 3 weeks in advance, especially for weekends." Prevents last-minute disappointment.
What payment methods do you accept?
Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), Bank Transfer, Cash. Momo is the most used in Ghana — lead with it.
What happens if it rains?
Canopy provision policy. Outdoor event risk management. Common concern in Accra during rainy season.

15
Final CTA Banner
Last push before the footer — re-engage scrollers who haven't booked yet
Re-converts
Full-width Colourful Banner
Bold background — deep green, gold, or a food photo. High visual contrast from the page body to break the scroll and demand attention.
Strong Emotional Headline
E.g. "Ready to Make Your Event Unforgettable?" or "Let Us Handle the Food. You Handle the Memories." Evocative, warm language.
Two CTA Buttons
"Book Your Date Now" (primary, bold) + "Chat on WhatsApp" (secondary, WhatsApp green). Caters to both decision styles.
Urgency Element
E.g. "Only 3 weekend dates left in December" or "Book now — slots filling fast." Creates gentle urgency without pressure.
F
Footer
Navigation, contact, social — utility + trust closing the page
Always present
Logo + Tagline
Brand logo and 1-line mission. E.g. "Serving Accra with love, one event at a time." Reinforces brand identity as the page closes.
Quick Navigation Links
Home · About · Packages · Menu · Gallery · Blog · Contact. Lets users jump anywhere without scrolling back to the top.
Contact Details
Phone number (click to call) · WhatsApp link · Email · Physical address in Accra. Include Google Maps pin link for the address.
Operating Hours
Mon–Fri: 8am–6pm · Sat: 9am–4pm · WhatsApp available 7am–9pm. Ghanaians check availability before calling.
Social Media Links
Facebook · Instagram · TikTok · YouTube. Facebook and Instagram are dominant in Accra. WhatsApp Business link too.
Payment Icons
MTN Momo · Vodafone Cash · AirtelTigo · Visa/Mastercard icons. Builds final payment confidence at bottom of page.
Copyright & Legal Links
© 2025 [Business Name]. Privacy Policy · Terms of Service. Minimal, one line at the very bottom.
Mini Newsletter Signup
Email input + subscribe button in the footer: "Get event deals and recipes." Low-friction way to build a mailing list.
+
Floating / Persistent Elements
Always visible helpers that follow the user as they scroll
Always on screen
WhatsApp Floating Button
Bottom-right green WhatsApp icon that stays fixed as users scroll. Tapping opens a pre-written WhatsApp message. Essential for Ghana.
Phone Call Button
On mobile, a phone icon on the bottom-left. Tapping dials directly. Many older Ghanaian clients prefer calling over chatting.
Scroll-to-Top Arrow
Appears after user scrolls down 400px. Lets users return to the top without scrolling the whole page back. Small UX win.
Sticky "Book Now" Bar (mobile)
On mobile screens only — a slim bar fixed at the bottom: "📅 Book Your Event · Tap to Start." Keeps the CTA always accessible on small screens.
Live Chat / Chatbot (optional)
A simple chatbot that collects event date, type, and guest count, then either books directly or passes to WhatsApp. Reduces phone call volume.
